Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade anytime?

Yes! Upgrade anytime and your new plan activates immediately after payment. Your existing jobs get extended to the new plan's duration.

What happens when my plan expires?

Jobs are marked expired and hidden from candidates. Renew to relist them instantly — all applicant data is preserved.

What are Contact Unlocks?

Each unlock lets you view a candidate's full contact details (phone + email). Unlocks reset daily and are pooled across all your active job posts. Unused daily unlocks do not carry over.

Is payment secure?

All payments are processed by Razorpay — India's most trusted gateway. We never store card details.

Do you offer refunds?

3-day refund policy if you're not satisfied. Contact support@hiretrack.co.in
