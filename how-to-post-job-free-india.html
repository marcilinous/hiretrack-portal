How to Write a Job Post That Works

1. Write a clear, searchable title

Use the title candidates actually search for. "Sales Executive" beats "Revenue Growth Champion". Include city for location-specific roles.

2. Include the salary range

Jobs with salary ranges get 40% more applications. Candidates hate wasting time on roles that can't meet their expectations.

Job Post Template:

[Job Title] — [Company] — [City]
We are [brief description]. Looking for a [role] to [2-line summary].
What you'll do: [4–5 bullet responsibilities]
What we need: [5–6 specific requirements]
Salary: ₹X–Y LPA

Getting Quality Applications

  • Use WhatsApp apply — candidates reach you directly
  • Post Tuesday–Thursday mornings — highest engagement
  • Respond within 24 hours — top candidates move fast
