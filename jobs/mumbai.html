Frequently Asked Questions

What types of jobs are available in Mumbai in 2026?

Mumbai offers diverse jobs in 2026 across finance and banking, IT, MIS Executive, HR, Sales, media and operations. As India's financial capital, it has especially strong demand for analytics, MIS and back-office finance roles.

What is the average salary for jobs in Mumbai?

Mumbai salaries are among the highest in India, roughly 15–20% above the national average, though the cost of living is also high. Entry-level roles start around ₹2.8L–₹4L per year, mid-level professionals earn ₹6L–₹12L, and senior finance/tech roles often exceed ₹20L.
