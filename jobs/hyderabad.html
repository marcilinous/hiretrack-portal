Frequently Asked Questions

What types of jobs are available in Hyderabad in 2026?

Hyderabad offers strong demand in 2026 for IT and software, MIS Executive, HR, Sales, pharma and life-sciences support, and customer operations roles. HITEC City and Gachibowli host both global firms and a growing base of SMEs and startups.

What is the average salary for jobs in Hyderabad?

Hyderabad salaries are close to the national average and slightly above for tech roles. Entry-level positions start around ₹2.5L–₹3.8L per year, mid-level professionals earn ₹5L–₹10L, and senior IT roles can exceed ₹18L per year.
