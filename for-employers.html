FAQ

Frequently asked by employers

How quickly will I get applicants?

Most jobs receive their first application within a few hours of being published. Roles with detailed JDs and competitive salaries typically receive 15–30 applicants within the first 48 hours.

Can I post jobs for free?

HireTrack offers a free trial that lets you post one job and unlock a limited number of candidates. After that, paid plans start at ₹499.

What cities do you cover?

HireTrack is strongest in Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Pune, Chennai and Delhi NCR, with growing coverage in tier-2 cities like Mysuru, Mangaluru, Indore and Coimbatore.

How is HireTrack different from Naukri?

HireTrack is built for SMEs that want to hire quickly without enterprise pricing. We pre-score candidates per role, give you direct WhatsApp access, and our plans start at a tenth of Naukri's enterprise pricing.

Is payment secure?

All payments are processed by Razorpay, India's most trusted gateway. We never store card details.
