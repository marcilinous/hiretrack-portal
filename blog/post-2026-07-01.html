Key Takeaways

  • Bengaluru's SME sector is expected to see a 15-20% increase in hiring
  • Data Analyst and MIS roles are in high demand, with typical salaries ranging from ₹6-12 lakhs per annum
  • Karnataka-based SMEs are looking for candidates with strong data analysis and visualization skills

Bengaluru, being the IT hub of India, continues to witness a surge in hiring trends, especially in the SME sector. This quarter, the city is expected to see a 15-20% increase in hiring, with a significant focus on Data Analyst and MIS roles. As per industry estimates, around 500-700 new job openings are expected to be created in these domains.

Hiring Trends in Bengaluru

Key Skills in Demand

  • Proficiency in tools like Excel, Tableau, and Power BI
  • Ability to work with large datasets and perform statistical analysis
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
