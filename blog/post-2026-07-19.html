Key Takeaways

Bengaluru, being the IT hub of India, has a thriving job market for Data/MIS professionals. On average, entry-level Data Analysts in Bengaluru can expect a salary range of ₹5-7 LPA, while mid-level MIS roles can range from ₹10-15 LPA. These numbers are typical estimates and may vary based on factors like company size, industry, and specific job requirements.

Salary Trends in Bengaluru

The salary trends for Data/MIS professionals in Bengaluru are influenced by the city's thriving startup ecosystem and the presence of many large corporations. As a result, there is a high demand for skilled professionals in this field, which in turn drives up salaries. According to industry estimates, the average salary for a Data Analyst in Bengaluru is around ₹6-8 LPA, while an MIS Executive can earn around ₹12-18 LPA.
