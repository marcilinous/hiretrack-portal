Bengaluru's Whitefield, Electronic City, and Koramangala are currently the hottest hiring spots for SMEs, with a high demand for Data Analyst and MIS roles. With typical salary ranges for Data Analysts falling between ₹6-12 lakhs per annum, these areas offer attractive opportunities for job seekers.
