Bengaluru's Hiring Hotspots

Published 21 July 2026 · HireTrack

Key Takeaways

  • Whitefield, Electronic City, and Koramangala are top hiring hotspots in Bengaluru
  • Typical salary ranges for Data Analyst/MIS roles: ₹5-15 lakhs per annum
  • Demand for data-driven professionals is high in Bengaluru's SME market
  • HireTrack simplifies hiring processes for SMEs

Bengaluru, the Silicon Valley of India, is home to numerous SMEs that drive the city's economy. Whitefield, Electronic City, and Koramangala are three prominent hiring hotspots, with a high demand for skilled professionals, particularly in Data Analyst and MIS roles. These areas are estimated to have around 500-700 SMEs, with a typical workforce of 50-200 employees.

Hiring Trends in Whitefield, Electronic City, and Koramangala

The hiring landscape in these areas is highly competitive, with SMEs looking for talented individuals to fill various roles. The average salary range for Data Analyst/MIS roles in these areas is estimated to be around ₹5-15 lakhs per annum, depending on experience and skills. Some of the key industries driving the hiring demand include IT, finance, and e-commerce.
