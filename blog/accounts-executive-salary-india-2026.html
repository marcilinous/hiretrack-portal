2026 Trends Shaping Accounts Executive Pay

  • GST + automation have made compliance the most billable accounts skill.
  • Cloud-first firms (Zoho/QuickBooks) pay a 10–15% premium over Tally-only roles.
  • CA Inter / CMA Inter candidates in accounts roles see the highest pay jumps.

Skills That Boost Accounts Executive Salary

High-value skills in 2026:

  • Tally + Zoho Books — adds ₹0.5–1.2 LPA
  • GST returns + reconciliation — adds ₹0.8–1.5 LPA
  • TDS, PT, PF compliance — adds ₹0.5–1 LPA
  • Audit support (statutory + internal) — adds ₹1–2 LPA
  • Advanced Excel + power query — adds ₹0.5–1 LPA
Stack 2–3 of these and you typically jump a salary band 12–18 months faster than peers who don't.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Accounts Executive salary in India in 2026?

Accounts Executives in India earn ₹2.2L–₹11L+ in 2026; GST/Tally + audit experience drive the premium. Most mid-level Accounts Executives sit in the ₹₹3.2–5L band, roughly 5–8% higher than 2025.

What is Accounts Executive salary per month in 2026?

A fresher Accounts Executive earns roughly ₹18k–₹27k per month in 2026, and senior Accounts Executives earn ₹63k or more per month depending on city and skills.

Which city pays Accounts Executives the most in India?

In 2026, Bengaluru and Mumbai consistently pay Accounts Executives 15–25% more than the national average, followed by Delhi NCR and Hyderabad.

Which skills raise Accounts Executive salary in 2026?

The biggest pay levers in 2026 are: Tally + Zoho Books, GST returns + reconciliation, TDS, PT, PF compliance — each of which typically adds the ranges shown in the skills section above.

Is Accounts Executive a good career in India in 2026?

Yes — Accounts Executive demand remains strong across SMEs and enterprises in India. With the skills called out above, the role offers clear paths into senior, lead and manager bands within 5–7 years.
