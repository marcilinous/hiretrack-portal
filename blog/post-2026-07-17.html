MIS Executive Demand in Karnataka SMEs

Published 17 July 2026 · HireTrack

Key Takeaways

  • Karnataka SMEs hire around 500-700 MIS Executives monthly
  • Typical salary range for MIS Executives is ₹4-8 lakhs per annum
  • Data analysis, reporting, and Excel skills are in high demand

Karnataka SMEs, particularly in Bengaluru, have a consistent demand for MIS Executives, with around 500-700 positions being filled every month. These professionals play a crucial role in providing data-driven insights to inform business decisions. Typically, the salary range for MIS Executives in Karnataka SMEs is between ₹4-8 lakhs per annum, depending on experience and skills.

MIS Executive Role and Responsibilities

The primary responsibilities of an MIS Executive include data analysis, report creation, and dashboard maintenance. They are expected to have strong analytical skills, attention to detail, and the ability to communicate complex data insights effectively. Proficiency in tools like Excel, SQL, and data visualization software is also essential.

Skills in Demand

  • Data analysis and interpretation
  • Report creation and presentation
  • Excel skills, including formulas and pivot tables
  • SQL and database management
  • Data visualization tools like Tableau or Power BI
