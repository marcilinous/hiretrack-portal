Excel, SQL & Power BI Skills in Demand

Published 13 July 2026 · HireTrack

Key Takeaways

  • Excel skills are a must for data analysis
  • SQL knowledge is required for data manipulation
  • Power BI skills are in high demand for data visualization

In Bengaluru's thriving SME market, Data Analysts and MIS professionals with expertise in Excel, SQL, and Power BI are in high demand. Employers typically look for candidates who can collect, analyze, and interpret complex data to inform business decisions. With the rise of data-driven decision making, these skills have become essential for businesses to stay competitive.

Key Skills in Demand

Bengaluru's employers are looking for Data Analysts with a strong foundation in Excel, including data manipulation, pivot tables, and chart creation. SQL skills are also highly valued, as they enable data professionals to manage and analyze large datasets. Power BI skills are increasingly in demand, as they allow professionals to create interactive and visual representations of complex data.
