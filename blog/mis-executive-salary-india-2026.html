MIS Executive Salary Trends 2026

Three trends are shaping MIS pay this year:

  • Automation premium: Employers increasingly expect MIS Executives to automate reports rather than build them manually — those who can are paid 15–25% more.
  • From reporting to insight: Roles are shifting from data entry toward analysis, blurring the line between MIS Executive and Business Analyst.
  • City convergence: Remote and hybrid work has lifted salaries in tier-2 cities, narrowing the gap with metros.

How AI, Excel & Power BI Skills Raise Your Salary

High-value skills in 2026:

  • Power BI / Tableau — adds ₹1.5–2.5 LPA
  • Advanced SQL — adds ₹1–2 LPA
  • Python for automation — adds ₹2–3 LPA
  • AI tools (ChatGPT / Copilot for reporting & analysis) — adds ₹1.5–3 LPA
  • Advanced Excel (Power Query, VBA) — adds ₹0.5–1.5 LPA
The biggest 2026 differentiator is using AI to speed up reporting and analysis. MIS Executives who pair Excel/Power BI with AI tools are being promoted to analyst roles faster.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MIS Executive salary in India in 2026?

In 2026, MIS Executive salaries in India range from about ₹2.7L per year for freshers to ₹15L+ for senior professionals with 5+ years of experience. Most mid-level MIS Executives earn ₹6L to ₹9.5L annually, roughly 5–8% higher than 2025.

What is MIS Executive salary per month in 2026?

A fresher MIS Executive earns around ₹22,000 to ₹32,000 per month in 2026, while senior MIS professionals earn ₹70,000 or more per month, depending on city and skills.

What is the average MIS Executive salary on Indeed India in 2026?

Job listings on Indeed India in 2026 show MIS Executive salaries ranging from ₹2.7L to ₹9L per year, with metro cities like Bengaluru and Mumbai at the higher end.

Which skills increase MIS Executive salary in 2026?

Power BI, advanced SQL, Python automation, and AI tools (such as using ChatGPT or Copilot for reporting) command the biggest premiums in 2026 — often adding ₹1.5L to ₹3L to an MIS Executive's salary.

Is MIS Executive a good career in India in 2026?

Yes. MIS Executives remain in high demand across banking, logistics, e-commerce and manufacturing. With AI and analytics skills, the role offers clear growth to MIS Manager, Business Analyst and Data Analyst positions.
