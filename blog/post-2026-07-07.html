Bengaluru's Hiring Hotspots: Whitefield, Electronic City, Koramangala

Published 7 July 2026 · HireTrack

Key Takeaways

  • Whitefield, Electronic City, and Koramangala are top hiring hotspots in Bengaluru
  • SMEs in these areas are looking for skilled Data Analysts and MIS professionals
  • Typical salary ranges for Data Analysts in Bengaluru SMEs are between ₹5-15 lakhs per annum

Bengaluru, being the IT hub of India, has numerous hiring hotspots that are home to a large number of SMEs. Whitefield, Electronic City, and Koramangala are some of the top hiring hotspots in the city, with a high demand for skilled professionals, particularly in the Data Analyst and MIS domains. These areas are preferred by SMEs due to their strategic locations, access to a large talent pool, and well-developed infrastructure.

Hiring Trends in Whitefield, Electronic City, and Koramangala

According to estimates, the demand for Data Analysts and MIS professionals in Bengaluru's SME sector is on the rise, with typical salary ranges between ₹5-15 lakhs per annum. The table below provides an overview of the hiring trends in these areas:
