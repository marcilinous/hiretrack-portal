Excel, SQL & Power BI Skills in Demand

Published 29 June 2026 · HireTrack

Key Takeaways

  • Excel skills are a must for data analysis and visualization
  • SQL skills are in high demand for data management and querying
  • Power BI skills are increasingly sought after for business intelligence

Bengaluru's thriving SME sector is driving demand for Data Analysts and MIS professionals with expertise in Excel, SQL, and Power BI. Employers typically look for candidates who can collect, analyze, and interpret complex data to inform business decisions. With the rise of data-driven decision making, these skills are becoming essential for businesses to stay competitive.

Excel Skills in Demand

Excel remains a fundamental tool for data analysis and visualization. Employers expect Data Analysts to be proficient in Excel, with skills ranging from basic data manipulation to advanced techniques like pivot tables, macros, and Power Query. Typical salary ranges for Excel-savvy Data Analysts in Bengaluru are between ₹4-8 lakhs per annum.

SQL Skills: A Must-Have

SQL skills are highly valued in the industry, as they enable Data Analysts to manage and query large datasets. Employers look for candidates who can write efficient SQL queries, optimize database performance, and ensure data integrity. Estimated demand for SQL-skilled professionals in Bengaluru is around 500-700 openings per quarter.

Power BI: Emerging Skill

Power BI is gaining traction in the industry, as it enables businesses to create interactive and immersive dashboards.
