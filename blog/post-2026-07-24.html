Typically, these roles offer a salary range of ₹4-8 lakhs per annum. To be successful, MIS Executives need to possess skills in data analysis, reporting, and visualization.

MIS Executive Role and Responsibilities

MIS Executives are responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining MIS systems to support business operations. They work closely with various departments to identify data requirements and develop reports to meet business needs.

Key Skills Required

  • Data analysis and interpretation
  • Reporting and visualization tools such as Excel, Tableau, or Power BI
  • Database management skills, including SQL and data modeling
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
