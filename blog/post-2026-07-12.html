Data/MIS Salaries Bengaluru: Entry vs Mid-Level

Published 12 July 2026 · HireTrack

Key Takeaways

  • Entry-level Data/MIS roles in Bengaluru offer salaries between ₹4-6 LPA
  • Mid-level Data/MIS roles in Bengaluru can command salaries ranging from ₹8-12 LPA
  • Salary ranges vary based on factors like company size, industry, and specific job requirements

When it comes to Data/MIS roles in Bengaluru, salary expectations can vary significantly based on experience level. Typically, entry-level positions in this field can offer salaries ranging from ₹4-6 LPA, while mid-level roles can command higher salaries, often between ₹8-12 LPA. These figures can serve as a general guide for both job seekers and hiring managers in the Bengaluru market.

Salary Ranges in Bengaluru

Bengaluru, being a hub for SMEs and startups, offers a wide range of salary options for Data/MIS professionals. The salary for an entry-level Data Analyst can start from around ₹4 LPA and go up to ₹6 LPA, depending on the company's size, the specific role, and the industry. For mid-level positions, such as Senior Data Analyst or MIS Manager, the salaries can range from ₹8 LPA to ₹12 LPA or more, considering factors like experience, skill set, and the company's budget.
