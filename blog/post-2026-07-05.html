Bengaluru Data/MIS Salaries: Entry vs Mid-Level

Published 5 July 2026 · HireTrack

Key Takeaways

  • Entry-level Data/MIS roles in Bengaluru offer salaries between ₹4-7 lakhs per annum
  • Mid-level Data/MIS roles in Bengaluru offer salaries between ₹8-15 lakhs per annum
  • Demand for Data/MIS professionals is high in Bengaluru's SME sector

Bengaluru, the IT hub of India, is home to a thriving SME sector with a high demand for Data/MIS professionals. Entry-level Data/MIS roles in Bengaluru typically offer salaries between ₹4-7 lakhs per annum, while mid-level roles can command salaries between ₹8-15 lakhs per annum. As of 2026, the market is witnessing a steady growth in demand for data-driven decision makers.

Salary Trends in Bengaluru

The salary trends in Bengaluru's Data/MIS market are influenced by factors such as industry, experience, and skillset. The following table provides an overview of typical salary ranges for Data/MIS roles in Bengaluru:

Role Entry-Level Salary (₹) Mid-Level Salary (₹)
Data Analyst 4-6 lakhs 8-12 lakhs
MIS Executive 5-7 lakhs 10-15 lakhs
Business Intelligence Developer 6-8 lakhs 12-18 lakhs

Key Factors Influencing Salaries

  • Industry: Salaries vary across industries, with finance and healthcare tend to offer higher salaries
  • Experience: Mid-level professionals with 2-5 years of experience can command higher salaries
  • Skillset: Proficiency in tools like Excel, SQL, and Tableau can increase salary potential
