About HireTrack

India's growing job portal — built in Bengaluru

🎯 HireTrack is on a mission to connect every job seeker in India with the right opportunity — faster, simpler, and smarter.

Who We Are

HireTrack is a full-stack job portal built for India's working professionals. We started with a focus on MIS, Data, and Analytics roles in Karnataka, and have since expanded to serve job seekers and employers across all industries and cities pan-India.

We are built by professionals who have experienced the friction of job hunting and hiring first-hand. Our goal is to make the process transparent, efficient, and human.

What We Do

HireTrack connects candidates with employers through a clean, fast, and mobile-first platform. We offer:

  • Free job browsing and applications for candidates
  • AI-powered interview preparation and career guidance
  • A profile strength scoring system to help candidates put their best foot forward
  • Affordable hiring plans for employers — from startups to enterprises
  • An executive network that helps employers get onboarded with a free trial

Our Values

Transparency
No hidden fees, no spam, no fake job listings. What you see is what you get.
Simplicity
Job seeking and hiring should be simple. We remove unnecessary complexity from both sides of the table.
Empowerment
We give candidates the tools — AI prep, profile scoring, real job data — to show up confidently.

Built in Bengaluru

HireTrack is proudly built in Bengaluru, India, with a vision to serve every city and every industry across the country. We are a small team with big ambitions — and we're just getting started.
